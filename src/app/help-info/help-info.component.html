Introduction

The eForms Designer Tool is part of Ontario Health's (OH) "Patients Before Paperwork" (PB4P) initiative. This tool facilitates the creation, publishing, and management of clinical and administrative form templates, in line with HL7 FHIR® SDC standards, with the goal of reducing administrative burdens and improving healthcare efficiency.

Designed for authorized users, including Builders and Approvers, the eForms Designer allows for the development and publishing of form templates in JSON format. It ensures data security and compliance with Ontario’s privacy regulations, operating within non-PHI environments to safeguard sensitive information.

 Getting Started

To use the eForms Designer Tool, you will need an Office 365 account. To request access, please email eformsdesigner@ontariohealth.ca, specifying whether you are requesting Builder or Approver access.

 Setting Up eForms Designer

Before you can use the eForms Designer Tool, ensure that your Office 365 account is set up. Once your access request is approved, you will be granted the necessary permissions to start using the too.
